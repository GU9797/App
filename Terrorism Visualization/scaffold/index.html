Terrorism in the US

The visualization illustrates terrorist attack patterns from 1970-1997, and contextualizes the recent litany of school shootings. The majority of attacks involve a Bombing/Explosion and are concentrated in urban centers. Notable attacks include the Oklahoma City bombing in 1995 (168 deaths), and the LaGuardia Airport bombing in 1975 (11 deaths).
